Summary



Coraline Jones moves to the pink palace with her very busy parents. While exploring she finds a door that leads to the other world where every element of it was made just for her. She could stay if she allowed buttons to be sewn into her eyes, she declines.. Soon she learns about the sinister motives of the Other Mother. She now has to save not only her own soul but her parents and three previous victims. With the help of her neighbors, some souls trapped by the other mother, her new friend Wybie she is able to escape her predicament.
